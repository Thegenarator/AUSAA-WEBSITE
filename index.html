welcome TO AUSAA KENYA

AUSAA Kenya is a non profit organization that brings together students and Associates from different Universities in Kenya

Missionary Work

The main role of AUSAA KENYA is to preach the Gospel and draw more people to Jesus Christ Matthew 28:18-20.

As Adventists we are called to preach the three Angels Message as recorded in Revelation 14:6-12 .

Home Visitations

AUSAA Kenya also plans for home visitations twice a year for the purposes of growing together and fellowships.

BIble Study

We also take sometime to gather together every quarter and have deeper Bible studies that benefit both members and the non members.

Prayer Nights

It's only prayers that has kept the group moving. Therefore, we dedicate a special night for thanksgiving .

our Mission work

AUSAA Kenya is an evangelical group that purely focuses on spreading the Gospel. Year after year we receive invitations from different parts of the country.

The only work left for Adventists and Christians is to evangelize and spread the gospel as we await the second coming of Jesus Christ.

The three angels messages in Revelation 14, and the 'fourth' angel of Revelation 18 contains God's final Three Angelswarning and call of mercy to the world, before the return of Jesus Christ. These messages are of such great importance that they have just as much significance, if not more, as the message Noah gave to the world before the flood. So any true believer should be greatly stirred by this. Time is running out friends! faith.
